About

Welcome to the inaugural Conference-X, a pioneering event in the field of X-ray imaging and studies of the Earth's magnetosphere. Taking place this year, Conference-X brings together experts, researchers, and enthusiasts to explore the fascinating world of X-ray-related studies.

With a specific focus on the intersection of X-ray imaging and the Earth's magnetosphere, Conference-X provides a unique platform for sharing insights, advancements, and breakthroughs in this specialized field. We are excited to welcome participants from the LEXI (Low-Energy X-ray Imaging) and SMILE (Solar wind Magnetosphere Ionosphere Link Explorer) projects, as they contribute their expertise and collaborate with fellow attendees.

Conference-X offers a rich program featuring keynote presentations, research paper sessions, interactive workshops, and networking opportunities. Engage with leading experts, exchange knowledge, and discover the latest developments in X-ray imaging techniques, data analysis, and their applications in understanding the Earth's magnetosphere.

Join us at Conference-X to expand your horizons, forge new collaborations, and contribute to the advancement of X-ray studies in the Earth's magnetosphere. Together, let's uncover the mysteries of our planet's magnetic field and delve into the exciting realm of X-ray imaging.

Conference-X is organized by the Boston University and the Catholic University of America.
