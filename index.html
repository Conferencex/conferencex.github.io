MagX

The MagX meeting brings together a diverse group of researchers working in the area of soft X-ray imaging and dayside magnetospheric science. The meeting will include talks as well as dedicated discussion periods with the objective to bring together the community to discuss past findings and coordinate future X-ray missions to optimize science outcomes.

2nd-4th, October, 2023 @ Mountain Run Conference Center

About

Welcome to the inaugural MagX. We aim to bring together researchers working on space X-ray measurements and missions such as the SMILE and LEXI to discuss the latest results and future prospects of these missions.
